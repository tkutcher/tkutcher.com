01 / What it is

What Anvilor does.

At its core, Anvilor is a forms platform. The interesting part for me is how far a “form” can go when it has useful logic, personalization, integrations, and good design.

01

Logic

Questions can branch, calculate values, and validate information based on the answers someone gives.

02

Personalized links

A form can be pre-filled or connected to a person, without asking them to make another account.

03

Connections

Payments, email, APIs, and integrations let the form participate in a larger process.

04

Managing responses

The people running a project need useful ways to see the data, follow up, and know what happens next.

02 / What I’m exploring

Learning by using it on real projects.

Anvilor is both a platform and a place to experiment. Using it for actual community projects helps reveal what is useful, what is missing, and what should be made reusable.

Understand

Start with the people

Learn what the organization is trying to do, who is involved, and where the current process is frustrating.

Build

Put the pieces together

Use forms, data, payments, messages, and integrations where they actually help.

Learn

Reuse what works

Take the useful lessons from one project and turn them into capabilities that can help with the next one.

01
